Korean BBQ Burgers

Description:

Description:

This recipe adapts the classic burger to add the tangy spike of Korean BBQ flavor.

The secret is adding the sauce to the meat before and during cooking.

Ingredients:

  • Potato bun
  • 85% lean ground beef
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Soy sauce
  • Turbinado sugar
  • Black pepper
  • Fresh garlic
  • Spring onion
  • Sesame oil
  • Mayonnaise

Steps:

  1. Combine soy sauce, sugar, black pepper, garlic (minced), and spring onion.
  2. Add 1 tbsp of mixture to mayo, along with 1/2 tsp sesame oil.
  3. In a large bowl, mix beef with 2 tbsp of soy sauce mixture.
  4. Form beef into patties. Before cooking, brush patties with soy sauce mixture.
  5. When ready, flip burgers. Brush again with soy sauce mixture.
  6. Toast bun. Server burgers with lettuce, sliced tomato, and mayo spread.
