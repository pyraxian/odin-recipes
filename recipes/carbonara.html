Bucatini Carbonara

Description:

Carbonara can be thought of as fancy Italian mac & cheese.

The bucatini traps hot air in its tube, which helps cook the egg without curdling.

Ingredients:

  • Bucatini
  • Eggs (whole and yolk)
  • Pecorino romano cheese
  • Black pepper
  • Guanciale

Steps:

  1. Cook the guanciale over medium high heat until well rendered.
  2. Shred the cheese.
  3. Cook the pasta in salted water.
  4. Mix the egg and egg yolk in a glass bowl. Combine with the cheese and black pepper.
  5. Drain the pasta. Transfer to the egg and cheese mixture and mix generously, allowing the egg to cook without curdling.
  6. Add the guanciale and serve.
