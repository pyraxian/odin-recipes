Cinnamon rolls

Description:

Cinnamon rolls are a delicious pastry to enjoy in the mornings.

Some find baking intimidating, but it really is as simple as following steps and proper measurement!

Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • White sugar
  • Brown sugar
  • Salt
  • Water
  • Baking powder
  • Yeast
  • Cinnamon
  • Butter
  • Confectioner's sugar
  • Egg

Steps:

  1. Mix dry ingredients.
  2. Mix wet ingredients.
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients.
  4. Cover and let rise for 30 minutes.
  5. Roll out dough into flat rectangle.
  6. Spread butter, then add cinnamon sugar mixture.
  7. Roll into cylinder, cut into slices.
  8. Put in pan and bake.
